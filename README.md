Fun with Triangles
============

## Live link : https://app.netlify.com/sites/fun-with-triangles-react-app/overview
## About
- Enter three angles of triangle check weather it can form a triangle.
- Area of trinagle: From base and height calculate area of tringle
- Area of triangle: From three sides of triangle calculate area of triangle using heron's formula
- Calculate hypotenous by taking 2 sides as input from user.
- Quiz: Fun quiz on properties of triangle to check your knowledge.
